Chappell Roan is an American pop singer and songwriter. She is known for her camp and drag queen influenced aesthetic. Her debut album "The Rise and Fall of a Midwest Princess released via island records has received critical acclaim. The album won Roan Best New Artist at the 2025 Grammy Awards as well as the Brit Awards International artist of the year. Roan's success has led her to be called a "queer pop icon", "a superstar in the making", and a "visonary performer". Additionally Roan has been credited in being a leading figure in the "lesbian pop renasissance".
