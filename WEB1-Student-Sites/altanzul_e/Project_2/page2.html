 Artist Statement

In pursuit of art and design as problem-solving instruments, I wanted to experience what this side of the world had to offer while giving it back in my own way. I wanted every visual element, color selection, and design layout to serve a defined purpose and adequate message to those who came across. The process of harmonizing the practicality of simplicity with the expressive nature of art has led me to embrace a minimalistic approach in my work.

I believe that in the simplicity of design lies the power to convey complex messages effectively. Through my minimalistic designs, I aim to distill ideas to their core essence, eliminating the unnecessary noise while retaining the beauty and impact of visual expression. It is in this delicate balance, where art transcends mere aesthetics and serves as a catalyst for profound change. I want my designs to be more than a simple feast for the eyes, but a thoughtful solution to inconveniences we face without losing the spirit of the design and looks.
