Already Dead - March 2024

This piece is a recreation of a screenshot from the 1952 movie 'Ikiru'. As a divergence from the original screen grab, my final piece emphasizes the darkness in his eyes and on his face, reflecting how he feels after his cancer diagnosis in the film.

PICCE Website Redesign - January 2024

During January of 2024, I took an Internet Applications Development course and the final project was to get together with a community partner, assess their needs, and complete a project for them. The project for our team involved interviewing our community partner, Partners in Campus and Community Engagement, and redesigning their current website. These are a few screenshot of the pages I personally designed, but every team member had at least a littel input in each page. Made in Figma.
