About

Mya Bair is a talented graphic designer with a passion for blending simplicity and practicality in her work. Specializing in illustrations, logo designs, and cartoons, Mya brings a unique, approachable style to every project she undertakes. With a keen eye for detail and an appreciation for clean, yet messily doodled designs, she creates visually engaging pieces that communicate clearly and effectively. Whether she’s crafting memorable logos or illustrating playful characters, Mya’s work reflects her belief in the power of design to connect with audiences in meaningful ways. Bair has a BA in Art, Graphic Design Track from Whitworth University.
