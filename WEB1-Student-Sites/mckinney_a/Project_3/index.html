What is Retro?

Retro web design revives past trends to add liveliness and flair to a site. There are many different Retro Web Styles including, Art deco style which is art from the 1920s, as well there is Vibrant retro designs; flashy design from the 1990s. Retrofuturism which is designs of the future as imagined by the 1950s.
