Artist Statement

Creating designs that are elegantly complex, yet boldly intriguing is my forte. I focus on crafting visual imagery that utilizes shapes, patterns, and colors to effectively convey intriguing imagery. I have discovered that matte, monotone colors are particularly powerful with conveying my designs to the way I imagine them. These hues of simple colors add intriguing shadows that convey the illusion of depth and emotion.

With each curve I aim to arouse curiosity, and with every shadow, I strive to weave a narrative. My work isn’t just art, it’s a quest to provoke thought and ignite curiosity. Drawing viewers in to investigate the profound, simple meanings concealed beneath the charm of monochromatic palettes. Through my skill of creating arousing intriguing designs through nuanced and engaging approaches, my designs are an invitation to interact and explore.

My Work

As a graphic designer, my work is rooted in storytelling, creativity, and visual impact. Over the past three years, I have developed my skills through many Art courses, refining my ability to create compelling and meaningful designs. My experience has allowed me to explore different aspects of graphic design, from Illustrations to Digital Animation, always pushing myself to experiment and improve.

I am particularly passionate about illustration and its role in visual storytelling. My goal is to specialize in creating artwork for TV shows and movies, contributing to there immersive worlds and narratives. I find inspiration in the unique and innovative design work these industries produce, and I strive to bring that same level of creativity and detail into my own projects.
