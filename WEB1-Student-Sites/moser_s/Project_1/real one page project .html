Why Whitworth?

I chose Whitworth because I got the most scholarship money compared to the other schools that accepted me. Another reason I'm here is because of how beautiful the campus is and its proximity to home.

Why Graphic Design?

Honestly, I had no clue what Graphic Design was until I came to Whitworth. The reason I chose this major is because I'm on a mission to turn my dreams into reality, and a Graphic Design major will help me get there. Also, after taking the Adobe Suite class, I just knew this was the right path.

Future Plans

My goal is to work with Nike for about five years, hopefully less. During my time at Nike, I hope to gain more knowledge about my craft as well as dial in on my strengths and weaknesses. After spending some time at Nike, I'll also start my own brand called EASY STREET. With my brand, I'll sell clothing designed by me as well as other designers' work. Lastly, when I'm 35, I want to open up community centers around America to help kids get the love and support they need.
