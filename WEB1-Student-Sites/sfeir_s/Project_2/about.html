Stephanie Sfeir is a Lebanese/American graphic designer and multimedia artist living in Spokane, Washington. Her work explores themes of identity, culture, and inner monologues using bold typography, collage-like elements, and portraiture. Sfeir has recently shown her work at Terrain 15, an annual juried multimedia art and music event celebrating artists in the Spokane area. Sfeir has BA in Graphic Design from Whitworth University.
