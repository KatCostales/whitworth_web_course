About Us

We are a small, independent coffee company located in North Spokane. Founded in 2030, we strive to serve the best coffee in Spokane at affordable prices.

Our founder, Rab Greenup, noticed a problem. There are no coffee shops open past 11pm located in Country Homes or any of the neighboring neighborhoods. And very few in the greater Spokane area that had an open lobby late at night.

And so we hope to fill in that gap. We hope that you enjoy the space as much as we do!
