According to Material.io (The official website for Material Design), "Material Design is an adaptable system of guidelines, components, and tools that support the best practices of user interface design. Backed by open-source code, Material Design streamlines collaboration between designers and developers, and helps teams quickly build beautiful products."

Built by Google, Material Design focuses on a clean, simplified look that prioritizes accessability and useability. Corners are often rounded, with high contrast but soft colors. :hover actions are common and clear, without being obtrusive. Icons are often used, with distinct meanings.

Some websites/companies that use Material Design include:

  • Google
  • Spotify
  • Discord
  • Duolingo
  • Microsoft Teams
